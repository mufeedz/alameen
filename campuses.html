Main Campus

Our Flagship Location

The Main Campus, established in 1985, serves as the heart of Al-Ameen School's educational mission. Located in the vibrant city center, this campus houses our middle and high school programs (Grades 6-12) in a spacious modern facility spanning 5 acres.

The campus combines traditional Islamic architectural elements with contemporary educational design to create an inspiring environment for learning and growth.

Annex Campus

Our Primary School Campus

The Annex Campus, established in 2005, is dedicated to our younger students (Grades 1-5). Located in the peaceful North District, this 3-acre campus was designed specifically for primary education with child-friendly spaces and secure environments.

The campus features colorful and engaging learning spaces, with special attention to creating an environment where young minds can flourish in both academics and character development.

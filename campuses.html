Main Campus

Our Flagship Location

The Main Campus, established in 1980, serves as the heart of Al-Ameen School's educational mission. Located in the vibrant city center, this campus represents 44 years of academic excellence and Islamic values.

Our unique educational model provides co-educational learning for younger students (up to 5th grade) and dedicated girls-only education for middle and high school (6th-12th grade), creating an optimal learning environment for each developmental stage.

Annex Campus

Modern Progressive Campus

The Annex Campus represents our progressive approach to education, serving students from Grades 1-12 in a fully co-educational environment. Located in the peaceful North District, this modern 3-acre campus was designed to foster collaborative learning and holistic development.

Our unique all-grades co-educational model creates a dynamic learning community where students of all ages learn together, building strong social skills and leadership qualities from an early age.

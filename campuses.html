Our Campuses & Facilities

Al-Ameen operates across two campuses in Erode, each equipped with facilities to support a holistic learning environment.

Karungalpalayam Campus (Main)

Address: 180, Cauvery Road, Lakshmi Narayan Nagar, Indra Nagar, Karungalpalayam, Erode – 638003

This campus houses Grades I to X and serves approximately 690 students. Key features include:

  • Comprehensive science labs (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Computer lab with internet access
  • Library
  • KID’S CORNER activity room
  • Spacious playground
  • Prayer hall
  • Average class size: 35
  • Student–teacher ratio: 35:1

Ellapalayam Campus

Address: Ellapalayam, Erode – 638107

This campus focuses on the Senior Secondary wing (Grades XI–XII) and features:

  • Smart classrooms with LCD projection
  • Advanced laboratory setups
  • Digital learning environment
  • Site of the "Masjidthul Qadir"

The grand opening of “Masjidthul Qadir” on the Ellapalayam premises strengthened ties between the school and local faith communities.

General Facilities & Services

To nurture both the physical and mental well-being of students across our campuses, Al-Ameen provides:

Laboratories

State-of-the-art Physics and Chemistry labs; Biology lab stocked with diverse specimens.

Technology

Computer labs with internet access; smart classrooms equipped with LCD projectors.

Prayer & Recreation

Dedicated prayer halls, spacious playfields, and a KID’S CORNER for younger learners.

Support Services

Access to clean drinking water, a fleet of transport vehicles, and optional hostel accommodations through partner facilities.

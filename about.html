Our Story

From humble beginnings to educational excellence, Al-Ameen School has been a beacon of learning, character development, and Islamic values for over four decades.

Founded in 1980 with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Islamic principles, we have grown from a small community school to one of the region's most respected educational institutions. Our journey reflects the unwavering commitment of our founders, the dedication of our faculty, and the trust of countless families who have chosen Al-Ameen for their children's education.

Our Journey

  • 1980: Foundation of Excellence

    Al-Ameen School opens its doors with just 50 students, guided by a vision to blend academic excellence with Islamic values. The school was established by visionary educators committed to providing quality education in a nurturing Islamic environment.

  • 1995: First Major Expansion

    The school expands to a new campus, introducing modern facilities including science laboratories, computer centers, and a comprehensive library. The curriculum is broadened to include advanced programs in sciences and humanities.

  • 2005: Recognition & Accreditation

    Al-Ameen receives state recognition for educational excellence and becomes affiliated with the State Board of Secondary Education. Introduction of specialized programs in Islamic Studies and Arabic language.

  • 2010: Growing Community

    Enrollment surpasses 2,000 students, reflecting the trust and support of the community. Launch of the Annex Campus to accommodate younger students with specialized facilities and age-appropriate learning environments.

  • 2020: Digital Innovation

    Implementation of cutting-edge educational technology and digital learning platforms. Launch of comprehensive online learning systems that proved invaluable during global challenges.

  • Today: Leading the Future

    Al-Ameen is recognized as a premier educational institution, nurturing young minds in an environment that promotes intellectual growth, character development, and spiritual excellence. With over 2,500 students and a 95% success rate, we continue to set new standards in educational excellence.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, character, and faith—fostering academic excellence, moral integrity, and a sense of responsibility to society and the Ummah. We strive to create an environment where every student can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and graduate as confident, compassionate, and capable individuals ready to make positive contributions to the world.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, uphold Islamic values, and contribute positively to the world. We envision graduates who are not only academically successful but also possess strong moral character, leadership qualities, and a commitment to lifelong learning and service to humanity.

About Al-Ameen School

Our history, mission, and commitment to excellence.

Our History

  • 1980: School Founded

    Al-Ameen School opens its doors with just 50 students, guided by a vision to blend academic excellence with Islamic values.

  • 1995: First Major Expansion

    The school expands to a new campus, introducing modern facilities and a broader curriculum.

  • 2010: 2,000+ Students

    Enrollment surpasses 2,000 students, reflecting the trust and support of the community.

  • Today

    Al-Ameen is recognized for nurturing young minds in an environment that promotes both intellectual growth and spiritual development.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, character, and faith—fostering academic excellence, moral integrity, and a sense of responsibility to society and the Ummah.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, uphold Islamic values, and contribute positively to the world.

Our Trust

Al-Ameen School is managed by the Al-Ameen Educational Trust, a dedicated body committed to upholding the highest standards of education and Islamic values. The Trust comprises visionary educators, community leaders, and philanthropists who have guided the school since its inception.

“Education is a trust, and we are its guardians.”

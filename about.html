Our History

Al-Ameen School was founded in 1980 with a mission to provide quality education that balances academic excellence with Islamic values. What began as a small institution with just 50 students has grown into a renowned educational establishment serving over 2,000 students across multiple campuses.

Our journey is a testament to our commitment to nurturing young minds in an environment that promotes both intellectual growth and spiritual development.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive education that balances academic excellence with Islamic values, nurturing students to become knowledgeable, principled individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that empowers students with knowledge, character, and skills necessary to excel in a rapidly changing global environment while maintaining their Islamic identity.

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in education, encouraging critical thinking and intellectual curiosity.

Islamic Principles

We integrate Islamic teachings and values throughout our curriculum and school life.

Community Service

We emphasize the importance of giving back to the community and helping others.

Global Perspective

We prepare our students to be responsible global citizens with respect for diverse cultures.

About Al-Ameen School

Established in [Year Established], Al-Ameen Matriculation Higher Secondary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where students achieve academic success while developing strong moral character rooted in Islamic principles.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens and compassionate leaders of tomorrow, contributing positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for its academic excellence, holistic development, and commitment to fostering a generation guided by faith and integrity.

Our Values

  • Faith (Iman)
  • Knowledge (Ilm)
  • Integrity (Akhlaq)
  • Excellence (Ihsan)
  • Community (Ummah)

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Lower Kindergarten (LKG) to Standard XII, following the Matriculation and Tamil Nadu State Board syllabus.

Kindergarten (LKG/UKG)

Play-based learning, foundational literacy and numeracy, introduction to Islamic studies.

Primary & Middle School (I-VIII)

Strong emphasis on core subjects, language development, science exploration, and moral education.

High School (IX-X)

Matriculation syllabus, preparation for board exams, career guidance foundations.

Higher Secondary (XI-XII)

State Board syllabus with various streams (Science, Commerce), intensive coaching for public exams.

Islamic Studies & Arabic

Integrated curriculum covering Quran, Hadith, Seerah, Fiqh, and Arabic language.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports, arts, literary clubs, competitions, and field trips for holistic development.

About Al-Ameen School

Established in [Year Established], Al-Ameen Matriculation Higher Secondary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment. We aim for academic success while developing strong moral character rooted in Islamic principles.

Affiliated to: Tamil Nadu State Board (Matriculation)

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens and compassionate leaders of tomorrow, contributing positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for its academic excellence, holistic development, and commitment to fostering a generation guided by faith and integrity.

Our Values

  • Faith (Iman) & Integrity (Akhlaq)
  • Knowledge (Ilm) & Excellence (Ihsan)
  • Community (Ummah) & Compassion

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Lower Kindergarten (LKG) to Standard XII under the Tamil Nadu State Board (Matriculation) syllabus.

Kindergarten (LKG/UKG)

Focus on play-based learning, foundational literacy & numeracy, and introduction to Islamic values.

Primary & Middle School (I-VIII)

Strong emphasis on core subjects, language development, science, social studies, and moral education.

High School (IX-X)

Matriculation syllabus focus, rigorous preparation for board exams, and foundational career guidance.

Higher Secondary (XI-XII)

State Board syllabus with streams: Science (Maths/Bio, Computer Science) & Commerce. Intensive coaching for public exams.

Islamic Studies & Arabic

Integrated curriculum covering Quran recitation & understanding, Hadith, Seerah, basic Fiqh, and Arabic language.

Extracurricular & Facilities

Includes sports (mention specific sports if possible), arts, literary clubs, competitions, field trips, computer lab, science labs, library.

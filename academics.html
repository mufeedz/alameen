Curriculum Overview

Al-Ameen School follows a robust curriculum that balances academic excellence with moral education. Our teaching methodology focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while nurturing Islamic values and character development.

Our curriculum is designed to meet and exceed the standards set by educational boards, preparing students not just for examinations but for life beyond school.

Key Features

  • Balanced academic and moral education
  • Focus on practical learning and application
  • Integration of Islamic values across subjects
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Preparation for competitive examinations

Grade-wise Curriculum

Primary (Grades 1-5)

Foundation years focusing on developing fundamental concepts, language skills, and moral values.

  • English, Tamil, Mathematics
  • Environmental Science
  • Islamic Studies & Moral Education
  • Arts, Music, and Physical Education
  • Basic Computer Skills

Middle (Grades 6-8)

Transition years building on fundamentals and introducing specialized subjects.

  • English, Tamil, Hindi
  • Mathematics, Science, Social Science
  • Islamic Studies & Arabic
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education & Arts

Secondary (Grades 9-12)

Advanced education preparing students for higher studies and career paths.

  • English, Tamil/Hindi
  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Computer Science, Commerce, Economics
  • Islamic Studies & Character Development
  • Career Guidance & Counseling

Special Programs

At Al-Ameen School, we offer specialized programs that enrich our core curriculum and provide students with unique opportunities for growth and development.

Islamic Studies

A comprehensive program integrating Quranic studies, Islamic history, and moral education, fostering spiritual growth alongside academic excellence.

Arabic Language

Specialized Arabic language instruction to build reading, writing, and conversational skills, connecting students with Islamic heritage and literature.

Computer Science

Advanced technology education including programming, digital literacy, and computational thinking to prepare students for the digital future.

Leadership Training

Developing future leaders through student council activities, community service, and character-building exercises focused on Islamic leadership principles.

Arts & Culture

Creative expression through visual arts, calligraphy, nasheed (Islamic songs), and cultural studies, celebrating Islamic artistic traditions.

Competitive Exam Prep

Specialized coaching for entrance examinations, scholarships, and national-level competitions to ensure academic and career advancement.

Evaluation System

Our comprehensive evaluation system ensures continuous assessment of student progress through various means.

Regular formative assessments through class tests, projects, and activities allow teachers to track student progress continuously and provide timely feedback and intervention.

  • Weekly class tests
  • Monthly progress reports
  • Project-based assessments
  • Classroom participation evaluation

Comprehensive assessments conducted thrice a year to evaluate student understanding of concepts covered during each term.

Term Period Coverage
First Term August Units 1-5
Mid Term December Units 6-10
Final Term March Comprehensive

We employ a comprehensive system to track student progress across academic years:

  • Detailed progress reports with subject-wise analysis
  • Parent-teacher meetings scheduled three times per year
  • Digital progress tracking system accessible to parents
  • Personalized improvement plans for students needing additional support

At Al-Ameen, we assess not just academic performance but also character development and Islamic values:

  • Behavior and conduct evaluation
  • Islamic studies proficiency
  • Community service participation
  • Leadership and responsibility
  • Akhlaq (moral character) development

This evaluation helps ensure our students grow to be well-rounded individuals with strong moral character alongside academic excellence.

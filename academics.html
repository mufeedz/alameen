Academics at Al-Ameen

We provide a comprehensive and balanced education affiliated to the Tamil Nadu Matriculation Board, focusing on academic rigor alongside strong moral and ethical values.

Curriculum & Affiliation

Al-Ameen is affiliated to the Tamil Nadu Matriculation Board and provides co-educational instruction in English from Grade I through Grade XII. Emphasizing a balance of strong academics and values-based learning, the school's curriculum integrates modern pedagogies to develop critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration skills.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Lower Kindergarten (LKG) to Standard XII.

Kindergarten (LKG/UKG)

Focus on play-based learning, foundational literacy & numeracy, and introduction to Islamic values.

Primary & Middle School (I-VIII)

Strong emphasis on core subjects, language development, science, social studies, and moral education.

High School (IX-X)

Matriculation syllabus focus, rigorous preparation for board exams, and foundational career guidance.

Higher Secondary (XI-XII)

State Board syllabus with streams: Science (Maths/Bio, Computer Science) & Commerce. Intensive coaching for public exams. Offered at our Ellapalayam Campus.

Islamic Studies & Arabic

An integrated curriculum covering Quran recitation & understanding, Hadith, Seerah, basic Fiqh, and Arabic language is a core part of our educational approach, nurturing faith alongside knowledge.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in holistic development. Our extracurricular offerings include sports, arts, literary clubs, competitions, and field trips which complement the academic curriculum. (See Campuses & Facilities for more details on available resources).

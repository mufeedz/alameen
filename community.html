Community & Co-curricular Activities

Al-Ameen fosters strong community bonds and encourages holistic development through various events and activities.

Exposition on Islam (2011)

Alumni organized a significant two-day event showcasing rare books on Islamic history and promoting interfaith harmony. The event was inaugurated by Hon. Justice Siddique.

Free Dental Health Camp (2020)

In collaboration with JKKN Dental College & Hospital, we organized complimentary dental checkups for students, staff, and parents, promoting health awareness within our community.

Alumni Network

Connecting past students to build a supportive community and preserve our legacy.

Alumni Association

The Al-Ameen Alumni Association strives to maintain connections among graduates and support the school's mission. Our alumni contribute to mentorship programs, career guidance events, and scholarships for current students.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to school facilities and library resources
  • Invitations to special school events
  • Networking opportunities with fellow alumni
  • Regular newsletters and updates

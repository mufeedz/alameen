Curriculum Overview

Al-Ameen School follows a robust curriculum that balances academic excellence with moral education. Our teaching methodology focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while nurturing Islamic values and character development.

Our curriculum is designed to meet and exceed the standards set by educational boards, preparing students not just for examinations but for life beyond school.

Key Features

  • Balanced academic and moral education
  • Focus on practical learning and application
  • Integration of Islamic values across subjects
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Preparation for competitive examinations

Evaluation System

Our comprehensive evaluation system ensures continuous assessment of student progress through various means.

Regular formative assessments through class tests, projects, and activities to track student progress continuously.

Comprehensive assessments conducted thrice a year to evaluate student understanding of concepts covered during each term.

We assess not just academic performance but also character development and Islamic values through behavior evaluation and community service participation.

Eligibility Criteria

Al-Ameen School welcomes applications from students who meet the following eligibility requirements:

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

  • Grade 1: Age 5+ years by June 30th
  • Basic reading and numeracy readiness
  • Performance in entrance assessment
  • Parent and student interview

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

  • Successful completion of previous grade
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Academic assessment in core subjects
  • Interview with academic coordinator

Secondary School (Grades 9-10)

  • Successful completion of Grade 8
  • Minimum 65% aggregate in previous academic year
  • Subject-specific entrance test
  • Interview with principal/vice principal

Higher Secondary (Grades 11-12)

  • Successful completion of Grade 10 Board Examination
  • Minimum 70% aggregate in Board Examination
  • Subject-specific minimum marks for chosen stream
  • Stream-based aptitude assessment

Additional Requirements for All Students

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous academic records (Report cards/mark sheets for the last two years)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school (original)
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Copy of Aadhaar card (or equivalent ID proof)
  • Medical record/certificate

* Admission is subject to availability of seats. Preference is given to siblings of existing students and children of alumni.

Admission Process

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Al-Ameen School:

1

Application Submission

Download and complete the application form. Submit it with all required documents and application fee.

2

Entrance Assessment

Complete the grade-appropriate entrance assessment to evaluate academic readiness.

3

Interview

Parent and student interview with school administrators to understand mutual expectations.

4

Final Enrollment

Upon selection, complete the enrollment process by paying the applicable fees and completing remaining formalities.

How to submit your application:

  • Download the appropriate admission form
  • Complete all sections of the form in clear handwriting or type
  • Attach all required documents as listed in the eligibility section
  • Submit the completed application in person at the school's admissions office
  • Pay the application fee of â‚¹500 (non-refundable)

Note: Incomplete applications will not be processed. All documents must be submitted together.

About the entrance assessment:

  • Grade-appropriate assessment in core subjects (English, Mathematics, Science)
  • Assessment duration: 60-90 minutes depending on grade level
  • No specific preparation is required, but students should be familiar with previous grade concepts
  • For Primary grades, basic readiness skills are assessed through interactive activities
  • Results are typically available within 5-7 working days

Assessment dates will be communicated after application submission. Students should arrive 15 minutes before the scheduled time.

The interview is an opportunity for mutual understanding:

  • Both parents/guardians should attend the interview along with the student
  • Interview duration: Approximately 20-30 minutes
  • Discussion topics include educational philosophy, expectations, school values, and student interests
  • For higher grades, students may be interviewed separately to assess their academic goals and interests
  • Original documents will be verified during the interview

Please arrive punctually for your scheduled interview. Rescheduling is subject to availability.

Final steps to complete enrollment:

  • Acceptance letters are sent via email and post
  • Fee payment must be completed within 7 days of receiving the acceptance letter to secure admission
  • Payment methods: Online transfer, demand draft, or at the school finance office
  • Complete and sign the enrollment agreement and code of conduct
  • Submit any additional documents requested by the admissions team

Note: Failure to complete fee payment within the stipulated time may result in the seat being offered to another candidate.

Fee Structure Overview

Al-Ameen School strives to provide quality education at affordable rates. The fee structure varies by grade level and includes various components:

Fee Components

  • Registration Fee One-time
  • Admission Fee One-time
  • Tuition Fee Quarterly
  • Term Fee Quarterly
  • Lab Fee Annual
  • Library Fee Annual
  • Computer Lab Fee Annual
  • Extracurricular Activities Annual

Payment Information

Detailed fee structures are available in the admission package and upon request from the admissions office.

Fees can be paid through the following methods:

  • Online bank transfer
  • Demand Draft in favor of "Al-Ameen School"
  • Cash payment at the school finance office
  • Cheque payment (subject to clearance)

A discount of 5% is available for full annual payment in advance. Sibling discounts are also available.

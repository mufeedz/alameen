Admission Requirements

Al-Ameen School welcomes applications from students who meet our eligibility criteria and can provide the required documentation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age-appropriate grade placement based on academic readiness
  • Previous academic performance demonstrating commitment to learning
  • Willingness to participate in school community and values
  • Basic proficiency in English and/or local language of instruction
  • Commitment to Islamic values and educational excellence

* Admission is subject to availability of seats. Preference is given to siblings of existing students and children of alumni.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (original and copy)
  • Previous academic records (last two years)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school (original)
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Copy of Aadhaar card (or equivalent ID proof)
  • Medical record/certificate

All documents must be submitted together. Incomplete applications will not be processed.

Process Overview

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Al-Ameen School and join our community of learners.

1

Application Submission

Complete our admission form with all required documents and application fee of ₹500.

2

Entrance Assessment

Grade-appropriate assessment in core subjects including English, Mathematics, and Science (60-90 minutes).

3

Parent & Student Interview

Meet with our administrators to discuss expectations, values alignment, and academic goals (20-30 minutes).

4

Admission Confirmation

Receive acceptance letter and complete enrollment with fee payment and agreement signing within 7 days.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Al-Ameen School believes in making quality education accessible to deserving students through various scholarship and financial aid programs.

Merit-Based Scholarships

  • Academic Excellence: Up to 50% fee waiver for top performers in entrance assessments
  • Board Toppers: Special scholarships for state and district level achievers
  • Continuing Excellence: Annual merit scholarships for consistent high performers

Need-Based Financial Aid

  • Financial Hardship: Partial to full fee assistance based on family income assessment
  • Orphan Students: Special provisions for students without parental support
  • Emergency Support: Temporary aid during unexpected financial difficulties

How to Apply for Financial Assistance

  1. Submit scholarship application form along with admission application
  2. Provide income certificates and supporting financial documents
  3. Complete family background verification process
  4. Appear for scholarship interview (if shortlisted)
  5. Receive scholarship decision within 10 working days

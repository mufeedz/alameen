Admission Process

Al-Ameen School welcomes applications from students seeking quality education integrated with Islamic values. Our admission process is designed to be thorough yet supportive, ensuring we find the right fit for each child.

We evaluate applicants based on academic readiness, character, and family commitment to Islamic education principles.

Download Application Form

Application Steps

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the application form along with required documents and the application fee.

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous academic records
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Application fee payment receipt
2

Entrance Assessment

Qualified applicants will be invited for an entrance assessment appropriate to their grade level.

  • Written assessment in core subjects
  • Reading and comprehension evaluation
  • Math and logical reasoning assessment
  • Basic Islamic knowledge (where applicable)
3

Family Interview

Parents and students are invited for an interview with school administrators to discuss expectations and values.

  • Discussion of educational philosophy
  • Alignment with Islamic values
  • Student's interests and aspirations
  • Parent involvement expectations
4

Admission Decision

After reviewing all components, the Admissions Committee makes a decision which is communicated to the family.

  • Acceptance letter
  • Fee structure details
  • Required enrollment documents
  • Orientation information

Eligibility Criteria

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

  • Grade 1: Age 5+ years by June 30th
  • Basic reading and numeracy readiness
  • Performance in entrance assessment
  • Parent and student interview

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

  • Successful completion of previous grade
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Academic assessment in core subjects
  • Student interview and family meeting

High School (Grades 9-12)

  • Strong academic record from previous school
  • Performance in entrance assessment
  • Teacher recommendations
  • Student essay and interview

Additional Notes:

  • All fees are subject to annual revision
  • Sibling discounts available (5% for second child, 10% for third child)
  • Scholarships available for deserving students
  • Transport fees vary by distance and are charged separately
  • Uniform, books, and stationery costs are not included in the above fees

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Al-Ameen School is committed to making quality Islamic education accessible to deserving students regardless of financial background. We offer various scholarships and financial aid options.

Available Scholarships:

  • Merit Scholarships: Based on academic excellence and entrance exam performance
  • Need-based Financial Aid: For families demonstrating financial need
  • Hafiz-ul-Quran Scholarship: For students who have memorized the Holy Quran
  • Sports & Extracurricular Excellence: For students with exceptional talents

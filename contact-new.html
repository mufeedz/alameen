Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about contacting us

The best time to visit our school is between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM on weekdays. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance through our Admissions Office to ensure staff availability for your visit.

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. Admissions-related questions may take up to 3 business days during peak enrollment periods.

Campus tours can be scheduled through our Admissions Office by calling +91 44 2445 7890 or by sending an email to admissions@alameen.edu. You can also use the contact form on this page to request a tour, mentioning your preferred date and time.

For all fee-related inquiries, please contact our Accounts Department at accounts@alameen.edu or call +91 44 2445 6789 (ext. 104). The Accounts Office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

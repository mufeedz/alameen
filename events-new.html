Science Exhibition

Date: October 15, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Location: Main Campus

Contact: Mr. Raheem (Science Coordinator)

The Annual Science Exhibition showcases the creativity and scientific prowess of our students across all grade levels. The event features innovative projects, experiments, and research presentations.

Highlights:

  • Interactive science demonstrations
  • Model exhibitions
  • Scientific research presentations
  • Innovation competitions
  • Guest lectures by prominent scientists

Schedule:

  • 9:00 AM - Inauguration
  • 9:30 AM - Exhibition opens to students
  • 11:00 AM - Science quiz competition
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch break
  • 2:00 PM - Exhibition opens to parents and visitors
  • 3:30 PM - Award ceremony
  • 4:00 PM - Closing ceremony

Parents and community members are cordially invited to attend and witness the scientific talents of our students.
